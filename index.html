Civil and Human Right Expo

There are many exhibits in a museum dedicated to our history. The purpose of a history museum is to provide facts and prevent us from making previous mistakes. I want you to feel inspired and knowledgeable about civil and human rights at the departure.

To improve as a society, we must determine what stops us from progressing. We plan to present the information undeniably. This exhibit is not meant to change the minds of the viewers but to have them challenge themselves to learn and possibly become an advocate for civil and human rights.

This guide will focus on the civil rights that various groups have fought for within the United States. While some of these rights, like the right to education, certainly overlap with human rights, we treat them as civil rights in most academic conversations. ABOUT

Civil and Human Rights are fundamental rights. Throughout the creation of life, these rights have been misinterpreted and eradicated. This project will address the issues that caused us to have laws to protect our natural human rights. It is a constant struggle to maintain these rights, and this scheme will bring attention to why we need to continue fighting. Solving this issue will make society a more pleasant place to live by protecting the freedoms you are given simply for being a human.
