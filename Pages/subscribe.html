About Us

Will Gatson (Founder)

Civil and Human Rights are fundamental rights. Throughout the creation of life, these rights have been misinterpreted and eradicated. This project will address the issues that caused us to have laws to protect our natural human rights. It is a constant struggle to maintain these rights, and this scheme will bring attention to why we need to continue fighting. Solving this issue will make society a more pleasant place to live by protecting the freedoms you are given simply for being a human.
