Current Issues

Human Rights Watch

According to hrw.org, we are still failing to meet human rights commitments. The most noticeable issue that we’re facing is systemic racism. Other significant issues that are occurring are poverty, drug policy, health, voting, and women's rights. the current cases that we face fuels our unwavering commitment to progressive principles, people’s movements and human rights as well as our creative use of the law as a positive force for social change.
